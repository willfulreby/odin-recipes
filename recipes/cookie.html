"Ultimate Cookie"

The "Ultimate Cookie" by Babish are some of the best cookies I have ever made. Unfortunately the complete recipe instruction in text are behind a pay-wall. You can however follow along with his YouTube video.

Video found

I would would highly recommend following along. If you want the cookies the day you make the dough, I would proof for the minimum time recommended. Howerver, if you would like what I feel is the best outcome, proof the dough for three days and the counter.

Ingredients

  • 226 g browned butter, room temperature
  • 276 g light brown sugar
  • 127 g granulated white sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 326 g all purpose flour
  • 2 tsp kosher salt
  • 1 tbsp hazelnut coffee grounds, finely ground
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 453 g chocolate chips
